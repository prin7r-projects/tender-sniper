Slide 5 — The bid-readiness score

Seven signals. Every score is auditable.

#SignalWeight
1Days-to-due-date (14-28d sweet spot)0.18
2Set-aside compatibility (SDVOSB / 8(a) / WOSB / HUBZone)0.16
3Past-performance similarity (matching NAICS)0.16
4Contracting officer history (with tenant or similar firms)0.14
5Attachment signal (SOW/PWS parsed vs amendment-only)0.12
6Knock-out terms (FedRAMP / IL5 / ITAR / clearance)0.12
7Dollar-band fit (against tenant min/ceiling)0.12

Rules-based. Auditable. Customers override per-lot; the override refines the model.
